Model Evolution

Six performances. One model the audience is teaching, season after season. The chart below shows how often each version of the machine would have won the audience vote, against the poems that actually won on the night. Ground.exe debuts at Currents New Media, Santa Fe — June 12, 2026.

Y-axis: pairwise win-rate against the human-voted winner of each theme, averaged over five generations per theme, judged by a frontier model from a different family than any candidate. Position bias mitigated by running every comparison twice with positions swapped. Methodology: see eval design.

What you're looking at

Every coloured dot is a performance you (or someone like you) attended and voted at. The line connects the model that opened that night to the model that closes the next one. The flat dashed lines are the big closed-source models — they don't change, because they aren't being trained on your votes.
